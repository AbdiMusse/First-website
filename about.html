A little about me

Who am I:

My name is Abdi-rahman Musse. I live in Manchester which is a vibrant and multi-cultural place. However, I was born in the Netherlands which I left at the age of 3. I had a very blessed and happy upbringing. I guess the only downside was that I was the only boy in my family, so I had no brothers to play with while growing up. On the plus side, I was very open and friendly, so I was fortunate enough to have many friends my age who I spent a lot of time with while growing up.

What do I do:

I am now a 20 year old computer science student at the University of Salford. Even though I have never done anything remotely related to computer science, I find it interesting and enjoyable. All my life, I have been studying biology, chemistry, and physics. However, I knew that science wasn't going to be the field that I will be working in. Therefore, when it was time for me to choose a course for University, I decided to go with computer science which I have not regretted ever since. At times it might be difficult as this is all new to me, however, I believe that to enjoy what you are doing, you must give it your best effort.

The modules that I am taking in my first year are:

  1. Computer System Internals and Linux
  2. Database Systems
  3. professional Development and Practices
  4. Programming 1
  5. Programming 2
  6. Web Development and HCI
